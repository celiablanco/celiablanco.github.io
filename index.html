About myself

Welcome to my corner of the web! I am a theoretical physicist looking for answers to some of the big question of astrobiology. If it's about the beginning or evolution of life in the universe, I'm all in.

Currently, I am a Research Scientist at the Blue Marble Space Institute of Science (BMSIS) and a Visiting Project Scientist at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) and a Graduate Research Fellow at the Spanish Center of Astrobiology (CAB, CSIC-INTA).

My research works explore the complex mechanisms and evolutionary processes that govern biological systems, for which I use theoretical approaches and computational tools. I’m all about teaming up with curious minds, so don't hesitate to reach out if you’d like to chat or collaborate!
